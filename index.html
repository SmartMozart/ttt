Tank Turn Tactics

How to play

  1. Press a key to select the mode
  2. Click a tile (or tank)
  3. Press enter

Keybinds

  • S - Shoot - click an enemy and press enter
  • H - Heal - click a friend (or yourself) and press enter (1 ap)
  • G - Give - click a friend and press enter to give an action point (1 ap)
  • M - Move - click a space to move to and press enter (1 ap)
  • U - Upgrade - press enter to increase range (3 ap)
